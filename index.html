ABOUT JACOB

WORK HISTORY

After completing my apprenticeship I stayed in Wenatchee and worked for a small salon for a short time. I then moved to Seattle to continue my learning and work along side a more diverse group of professionals. I stayed in seattle for about a year before moving back to central WA where I was self employed for roughly 6 years. My most recent place of work has been at a salon in Spokane called House Of Pop where I have been working the last year.
